Our Jewelry

Permanent jewelry is custom-fit, welded-on jewelry designed to be lived in — without clasps, snaps, or the need to take it off. These delicate, high-quality chains are seamlessly fused together for a minimalist look that symbolizes connection, intention, and lasting style. Whether you're marking a milestone, celebrating friendship, or simply treating yourself, permanent jewelry is the ultimate wearable keepsake.

At Forever Charmed, we specialize in:

  • Custom-fit, welded bracelets, anklets, necklaces, rings, body chains, hand chains, & your next creation!
  • A curated selection of 14k gold-filled, .925 sterling silver, and some enamel and two-tone chains — 10/14K gold offered upon request
  • Charms to personalize your piece — from initials and birthstones to evil eyes and hearts
  • Private parties, pop-ups, and one-on-one appointments for a truly personal experience

Each piece is thoughtfully selected and lovingly applied to celebrate the charm of every moment — forever.

Our Story

Hi, I’m Kayla! I started Forever Charmed with the mission to bring timeless, meaningful jewelry to life — quite literally. What began as a spark of creativity turned into a business that celebrates connection, memory, and elegance. I am a product manager and multi-hyphenate creative with a passion for design, and I’ve always believed that jewelry should be more than just an accessory; it should be a reflection of who you are and what you cherish.

As a lover of all things beautiful, I wanted to create jewelry that not only looks stunning but also carries a story. Each piece is custom-welded to fit you perfectly, symbolizing the unique bond we share with our loved ones and the moments that define us. I discovered the magic of permanent jewelry while planning my own wedding, and I was captivated by the idea of having a permanent momento — something that would last a lifetime and remind me of this wonderful moment in time. That’s why I’m dedicated to crafting pieces that are as special as the memories they hold.

Join me on this journey of elegance and charm — let’s create something beautiful together!
